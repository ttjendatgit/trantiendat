Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Deepen understanding of AWS networking services, attend AWS Cloud Day Vietnam](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Understand the scaling process for EC2 instances](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Begin foundational learning of Amazon Virtual Private Cloud (VPC) and its core components](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Translate and gain a solid understanding of two important technical blog posts](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Learn to set up Hybrid DNS using Route 53 Resolver](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Learn for the exam](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Prepare and do the exam](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Research and identify suitable AWS services for building a weather API](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Focus on enhancing and optimizing the performance of the weather API](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Focus on serverless and AI/ML topics relevant to project development](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Document all events participated in during the internship and finalize the worklog documentation.](1.12-week12/)
